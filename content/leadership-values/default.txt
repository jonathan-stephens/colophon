Title: Leadership Values

----

Text:

## Inclusive
I value diverse perspectives, creating safe spaces, and ensuring all voices are heard.

## Accountable
I take responsibility for my actions, manage expectations transparently, and follow through.

## Situational
I meet people where they are, adapting my methods to fit the needs of the team & organization.

## Collaborative
I believe in collective power, leveraging diverse strengths through collaboration and coalition.

## Adaptive
I embrace change with curiosity, actively listen to feedback, and adjust course as needed.

## Iterative: I innovate by embracing a test-and-learn, growth mindset while experimenting with new approaches.
